#Variable definition (column names)
Participant.Public.ID : Participant ID (public) [MASKED FOR ID PROTECTION].
Participant.Private.ID : Participant ID (private).
Participant.Starting.Group : Group (testing session per Country).
ParticipantID : additional auxiliary ID produced by the test script locally.
WhichPhase : Decision phase (Training = 0 / Learning = 1 / Transfer = 2 / Lottery = 3)
TrialNumber : trial number (by phase)
Condition : decision contexts, so that "condition number = [reward probability, reward magnitude]"
	1 = [0.25,10] vs [0.75,10];
	2 = [0.25,10] vs [0.75,10];
	3 = [0.25,1] vs [0.75,1];
	4 = [0.25,1] vs [0.75,1];
	5 = [0.75,1] vs [0.75,10];
	6 = [0.25,1] vs [0.25,10];
	7 = [0.25,1] vs [0.75,10];
	8 = [0.75,1] vs [0.25,10];
	9 = [1,1] vs [0.25,10]; #from here down, Lottery decisions only
	10 = [1,1] vs [0.5,10];
	11 = [1,1] vs [0.75,10];
	12 = [1,1] vs [1,10];	
SymbolLeft : Which of the two symbols of the pair (transiently identified with an integer) is on the left?
SymbolRight : Which of the two symbols of the pair (transiently identified with an integer) is on the right?	
ChoseLorR : Did the participant choose the symbol on the left (-1) or on the right (1)?
ChoseGoodorBad : Did the participant choose the value maximizing option? (0/1) 
RewardGoodorBad : Is there a reward for the chosen option in this trial? (Attributed stochastically) (0/1)	
ResponseTime: click time in ms
Reward: cumulative reward (in points)
OtherRewardGoodorBad: Is there a reward for the option NOT chosen in this trial? (Attributed stochastically) (0/1)
Session	: Session index [-1 = training]
Proba1: Probability for option 1
Proba2: Probability for option 2
Magnitude1: Magnitude for option 1
Magnitude2: Magnitude for option 2
Valence: DEPRECATED
Information: DEPRECATED
Option1: Chosen symbol for option 1
Option2: Chosen symbol for option 2
InvertedPosition: DEPRECATED (is the option that was to the left flipped to the right?)
ChoiceTime: timing of choice in system time
TrialPerCond: cumulative trial count
Language: language in which the experiment and instructions are presented.
Age: participant age.
Sex: participant gender.
FinalPayment: final reward
CRT: score of 7-item Cognitive Reflection Test
CvIHorInd: score of Horizontal Individuality 
CvIVerInd: score of Vertical Individuality
CvIHorCol: score of Horizontal Collectiveness
CvIVerCol: score of Vertical Collectiveness
RelIntellect: score of Intelectual dimension (Centrality of Religiosity inventory)
RelIdeology: score of Ideology dimension (Centrality of Religiosity inventory)
RelPub: score of Public life dimension (Centrality of Religiosity inventory)
RelPriv: score of Private life dimension (Centrality of Religiosity inventory)
RelExp: score of Religious experience dimension (Centrality of Religiosity inventory)
TIPIAgr: Agreeableness (10 Item Personality Inventory)
TIPIOpen: Openness (10 Item Personality Inventory)
TIPIEmostab : Emotional Stability (10 Item Personality Inventory)
TIPICons : Conscienciousness (10 Item Personality Inventory)
TIPIExtra : Extraversion (10 Item Personality Inventory)
SESInfant : Perception of childhood socio-economic status score
SESAdult : Perception of adult socio-economic status score
SESsubj : Perception of position in social hierarchy
deltaEV : difference in expected value between choices in a decision context
deltaEVfactor : difference in expected value between choices in a decision context (doubled, for later transformation as a factor)
Country : country of participant
